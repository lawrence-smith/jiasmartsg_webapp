Recommended Setup

Scenario: Welcome Home Lighting

Goal: Turn on lights automatically when you enter your home.

What You'll Need

Device Purpose Brand Options Est. Price (SGD)
Motion Sensor Detect presence at entrance Aqara $30
Smart Bulb Light that responds to automation IKEA, Aqara $20–$40
Smart Hub Connects all devices Aqara Hub M2 $80

Estimated Total Cost: $130–$150 SGD

How It Works

Motion sensor detects your presence and signals the hub to switch on the smart bulb. Seamless automation the moment you step in.

Setup Tips

  • Place motion sensor near entrance at chest height
  • Use Aqara app or Apple Home/Google Home to configure automation
  • Ensure Wi-Fi is stable near your entry point
